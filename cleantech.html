Transforming Waste Management with Transfer Learning

About CleanTech

CleanTech leverages cutting-edge AI and transfer learning to revolutionize waste management. By using pre-trained models, we can accurately classify and sort waste, making recycling more efficient and reducing environmental impact.

How Transfer Learning Helps

Transfer learning allows us to use knowledge from existing AI models (like image classifiers) and adapt them to recognize different types of waste. This means faster development, higher accuracy, and less data required for training.

Benefits

  • Improved waste sorting accuracy
  • Reduced landfill waste
  • Enhanced recycling processes
  • Lower environmental footprint
